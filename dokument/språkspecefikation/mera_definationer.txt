# Types

## Primitives

### Char

a single character in UTF-8. starts with ' and ends with '.

### Integer

a whole number. consists of a sequence of numbers.

### Float

a floating point number. consists of a sequence of numbers followed by a dot and at least one other number. 

### Boolean

a true or false value. Always denoted by true or false in lowercase. 

## Containers

### String

A combination of Chars. starts with " and ends with ". 

### Arrays

Constists of elements. starts with [ and ends with ].
